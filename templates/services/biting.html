Biting

Cyclists – postmen – waitresses on cafés – other male dogs - often pose a significant threat to your well-being. How do you know if that’s the case? You usually don’t! But don’t worry - I can tell. No more barging into your personal space. No more hello-saying. I start by barking and growling. In this way I also prevent them from trying to negotiate. If the keep approaching I bite. You will not be bothered by uninvited (or invited) guests ever again.
